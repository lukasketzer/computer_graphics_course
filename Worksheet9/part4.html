Comparison: Projection Shadows and Shadow Mapping

Projection Shadows

  • Pros:
    • Simple
    • Fast
    • No additional textures used
    • Works well for flat surfaces
  • Cons:
    • Restricted to flat surfaces
    • Unrealistic

Shadow Mapping

  • Pros:
    • Allows complex geometry
    • Supports dynamic shadows
    • More realistic
  • Cons:
    • Complex implementation
    • Performance-intensive
